Executive Summary / ملخص تنفيذي

What HYSA is

HYSA is a beta social media platform combining feed posts, stories, reels, profiles, direct messages, voice messages, media sharing, AI assistance, and creator/community features.

HYSA منصة اجتماعية في مرحلة بيتا تجمع بين المنشورات، الستوري، الريلز، الملفات الشخصية، الرسائل الخاصة، الرسائل الصوتية، مشاركة الوسائط، مساعد الذكاء الاصطناعي، وميزات المجتمع وصناع المحتوى.

Why it matters

Algeria has a visible gap for polished local social products. HYSA can use national pride, youth communities, universities, tech pages, and creator culture to grow locally before attempting any global competition.

يوجد في الجزائر فراغ واضح لتطبيق اجتماعي محلي مصقول. يمكن لـ HYSA الاستفادة من المشاريع الجزائرية، المجتمعات الشبابية، الجامعات، الصفحات التقنية، وثقافة صناع المحتوى للنمو محلياً.

Technical Architecture / البنية التقنية

Backend-secured product stack

The safest architecture keeps secrets and heavy logic on the backend. Mobile and web clients call HYSA APIs; the backend handles authentication, rate limits, media validation, AI calls, and response normalization.

MobileReact Native with Expo SDK 54
BackendNode.js + Express central API
StoragePostgreSQL + JSON fallback/hybrid
MediaCloudinary for image/video hosting
DeploymentRender web service
BuildsEAS Android APK
AIGoogle Gemini through backend only

HYSA AI Portfolio / ذكاء HYSA

Gemini-backed assistant, not frontend secrets

HYSA AI is integrated through POST /api/ai/chat and uses Gemini from the backend. It supports captions, hashtags, bio ideas, post improvement, simple Q&A, and conversational help in English, Arabic, and Darja-style mixed language.

  • Backend-only API key handling
  • Gemini 2.5 Flash production direction
  • Rate limiting and input length caps
  • Diagnostics for fallback, timeout, and Gemini status
  • Mobile AI screen and web AI panel
  • Copy/regenerate/typing-style user experience

Messaging & Realtime Foundation / الرسائل وأساس التحديث اللحظي

Safe polling beta strategy

HYSA currently uses safe polling rather than WebSockets. Messages refresh while the screen is focused, typing indicators appear, active states show presence, and read/delivered status is being built progressively.

Current DM direction

  • 4-second focused chat polling
  • 5-second DMThreads refresh while focused
  • Typing start/stop endpoint
  • Active now / active recently metadata
  • Delivered/Seen foundation
  • Timer cleanup on blur/unmount
  • No aggressive scroll yanking

Algeria-First Growth Strategy / استراتيجية نمو موجهة للجزائر

Local youth platform before global competition

HYSA should not start by competing globally with Instagram or TikTok. Its realistic path is to become a local Algerian youth platform with a story: made by a young Algerian developer, polished enough to try, and community-driven enough to share.

Launch pages on TikTok, Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube Shorts.
Position as “modern Algerian social platform”.
Target one university, city, or community first.
Recruit micro-creators and local tech pages.
Use beta invites, challenges, and local trends.
Avoid claiming to replace Facebook or Instagram.

Commercial Potential / الإمكانات التجارية

As a beta MVP without proven users or revenue, HYSA is most valuable as a working codebase, demo product, and local social app foundation. The value increases significantly only when traction appears: users, retention, creator activity, and community adoption.

Investor/client story
